'''Definition:normctsdualqHahnptilde'''
The LaTeX DLMF and DRMF macro '''\normctsdualqHahnptilde''' represents the normctsdualqHahnptilde.

This macro is in the category of polynomials.

In math mode, this macro can be called in the following ways:

:'''\normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}''' produces <math>{\displaystyle \normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}}</math><br />
:'''\normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}''' produces <math>{\displaystyle \normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}}</math><br />
:'''\normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}@@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}''' produces <math>{\displaystyle \normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}@@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}}</math><br />

These are defined by
<math>{\displaystyle
{\tilde p}_n(x):=\normctsdualqHahnptilde{n}@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}=\frac{a^n\ctsdualqHahn{n}@{x}{a}{b}{c}{q}}{\qPochhammer{ab,ac}{q}{n}}
}</math>

'''Definition:GaussDistQ'''
The LaTeX DLMF and DRMF macro '''\GaussDistQ''' represents the GaussDistQ.

This macro is in the category of integer valued functions.

In math mode, this macro can be called in the following ways:

:'''\GaussDistQ''' produces <math>{\displaystyle \GaussDistQ}</math><br />
:'''\GaussDistQ@{x}''' produces <math>{\displaystyle \GaussDistQ@{x}}</math><br />

These are defined by
<math>{\displaystyle
\GaussDistQ@{x}:=\frac{1}{2}\erfc@{\frac{x}{\sqrt{2}}}
}</math>
